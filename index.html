I am a master's student in Management Science and Engineering at Tongji University, with research interests in corporate non-market strategy, empirical supply chain, and political economy. I hold a B.A. in Engineering Management, also from Tongji University.

My first-author paper on smart city policy and construction innovation is published in Engineering, Construction and Architectural Management. I also work on projects related to stakeholder feedback, innovation in state-owned enterprises, and political affinity in international business.

I have presented my research at POMS and IACMR conferences. I am currently a research assistant at the Advanced Institute of Business, working with Prof. Jie Peng. I also serve as a reviewer for Engineering, Construction and Architectural Management.

My technical toolkit includes Stata, R, Python, LaTeX, and SPSS. I am fluent in English (IELTS 7.0) and a native speaker of Chinese. I also write creatively as a member of the Anhui Writers Association.
